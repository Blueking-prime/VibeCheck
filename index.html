ABOUT

Why does this exist?

Have you ever been in the shower, singing along and vibing to the some banger EDM from your saved tracks, then next thing you're being lulled to sleep by a classical piece?
Or have you ever been in the middle of a workout session, listening to some pumped songs, then next thing your listening to religious music?
Yeah, I've been there too, and it sucks. I created this app to stop stuff like that.
This started as a school project for Holberton School, so thanks to them for giving me the vibe to actually start working on this
